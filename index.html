Arabica Showcase

Distinct origins, curated lots

Each growing region contributes its own terroir—earthy cocoa from Mandheling, syrupy sweetness from Gayo, and bright citrus from Silintong. Build signature blends with consistent supply.

Silintong

Elevation 1,400–1,500 masl with washed processing; floral aromatics and grapefruit acidity that shine in light roasts.

Mandheling

Classic wet-hulled body with notes of dark chocolate, cedar, and spice—ideal base for espresso blends.

Karo Simalem

Balanced cup featuring brown sugar sweetness and toasted almond finish sourced from Northern Sumatra farmer cooperatives.

Sipirok

Clean, syrupy mouthfeel with hints of tropical fruit; versatile for both filter and omni-roast approaches.

Gayo

Organically cultivated, honey-processed microlots with jasmine fragrance and caramel sweetness prized by specialty cafés.
