Arabica Showcase

Distinct origins, curated lots

Each growing region contributes its own terroir—palm sugar sweetness from Sidikalang, volcanic spice from Karo Simalem, syrupy fruit from Samosir and Sipirok, and bright citrus from Lintong. Build signature blends with consistent supply.

Sidikalang

Velvety body with palm sugar sweetness and herbal cacao finish from shade-grown plots near Lake Toba.

Karo Simalem

Balanced cup featuring brown sugar sweetness and toasted almond finish sourced from Northern Sumatra cooperatives.

Samosir

Wet-hulled Mandheling-style lots with cedar spice, black tea florals, and syrupy body suited for espresso.

Sipirok

Clean, syrupy mouthfeel with hints of tropical fruit; versatile for both filter and omni-roast approaches.

Lintong (Silintong)

Elevation 1,400–1,500 masl with washed processing; floral aromatics and grapefruit acidity that shine in light roasts.
